I have covered local events in SoCal since I was seven years old. Some of the stories I've reported on included the 2016 election, a hotel workers strike in San Francisco, and a train-versus-pedestrian accident. My latest video can be found below.

Newspaper Experience

I spent my eighth grade year contributing to my middle school's newspaper. I wrote and co-wrote several sports articles for them. In high school, I spent two years as a varsity basketball reporter for my school newspaper, The Loyalist. Some of my recent articles for them can be found in the Sports section. My hard work paid off after I was chosen to be a Sports Editor for the 2020-21 staff. Recently, I was chosen to be the Managing Editor and Creative Director for the 2021 Junior Edition, a paper produced exclusively by 9th-11th graders.

Medium Blog

In November of 2019, I started a blog on Medium called The Voice of Truth. Originally started as a news blog on WordPress, it grew to a blog about the NBA and MLB. With professional sports on hiatus, I decided that I would write about my programming journey on the site.
