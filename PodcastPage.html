The Boundless & Ballin' Podcast

The Boundless & Ballin' Podcast is a show meant to discuss all things NBA. From mock drafts to deep dives, I cover the most important storylines in the league and recap some of the biggest games of the week. Available on Anchor and Spotify. New episodes every other Saturday.

About the Host

My name is Jordan Pagkalinawan. I've have had several years of experience as a sports student journalist. At my school, Loyola High School of Los Angeles, I served as a beat writer for its newspaper as a Freshamn and Sophomore. Now, as a junior, I am one of its Sports Editors. I've also had the pleasure of calling basketball broadcasts for the John Malloy Broadcast Club over the past two years.
